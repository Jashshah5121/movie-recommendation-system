# 📖 User Manual: MovieHub AI

Welcome to MovieHub AI! This guide will walk you through how to use the platform to discover your next favorite movie using traditional browsing and our advanced Natural Language AI.

## 1. Getting Started (Onboarding)
When you first open MovieHub AI, you will be greeted with an onboarding screen.

![Onboarding Screen](./Images/Onboarding.png)

* **Action:** Select 3 to 5 of your favorite genres (e.g., Action, Sci-Fi, Thriller) from the list provided.
* **Why?** This solves the "cold start" problem. Instead of an empty dashboard, MovieHub will instantly generate a personalized feed based on your initial selections.

## 2. Navigating the Homepage
The homepage is your central hub for movie discovery.

![Homepage Overview](./Images/Homepage&Recommendation.png)

* **Hero Banner:** At the top, you will see a dynamically updated banner featuring the most popular movie right now, complete with a rating and a quick link to watch the trailer.
* **Carousels:** Scroll down to browse horizontally through different categories, including **Trending**, **Popular**, **Upcoming**, and **Top Rated** movies. These are updated in real-time.

## 3. Searching for Movies
MovieHub AI offers three different ways to search:

### A. Standard Search & Live Autocomplete
* Click the Search bar at the top of the screen.
* As you type a movie title, a dropdown will appear instantly showing the top matches, including their movie posters and release years.
* Click any suggestion to go directly to that movie's page.

### B. AI Semantic Search (Natural Language)
This is MovieHub's most powerful feature. You do not need to know the name of a movie to find it.

![AI Semantic Search](./Images/AI&NLP-based_recommendation.png)

* In the search bar, describe the movie naturally.
* **Examples:**
  * *"Movie where toys come alive"*
  * *"Space travel and time dilation"*
  * *"Tom Cruise action movie"*
* The AI will understand the context of your sentence and return the best matches, explaining exactly *why* it recommended them to you (e.g., *"95% Match: Space Adventure"*).

## 4. Advanced Discover & Filtering
If you want to narrow down your options using specific criteria, navigate to the **Discover** page.

![Discover Page Filters](./Images/DiscoverMovies.png)

* Use the sidebar/dropdowns to filter by:
  * **Genre** (e.g., Horror, Comedy)
  * **Minimum Rating** (e.g., 8.0+)
  * **Language** (e.g., English, Spanish)
* The results will update instantly as you adjust the filters.

## 5. The Wishlist System
MovieHub allows you to save movies for later, and uses these saves to improve your future recommendations.

![Wishlist and Recommendations](./Images/wishlist_recommendation.png)

* **How to Add:** Click the **Heart Icon (❤️)** on any movie card. It will animate to a filled state.
* **Viewing your Wishlist:** Click the Wishlist icon in the navigation bar to see all your saved movies.
* **Dynamic Recommendations:** When you visit your personalized recommendation tab, the AI analyzes everything in your Wishlist to find new movies that perfectly blend your unique tastes.

## 6. Movie Details & Trailers
Clicking on any movie poster will take you to its dedicated Details Page.

![Movie Details Page](./Images/MovieDetails.png) 

* Here you can read the full plot overview, see the cast and director, and view user ratings.
* **Trailers:** Click the "Watch Trailer" button to instantly open the official YouTube trailer in a new tab.
* **Similar Movies:** At the bottom of the details page, you will find a curated list of similar movies to keep your discovery journey going.
